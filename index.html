About CAF

Founded in 2010, CAF has been serving premium coffee and freshly baked goods to our community for over a decade. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where people can gather, work, and enjoy exceptional quality beverages and food.

We source our coffee beans from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to bring out their unique flavors. All our pastries and desserts are baked fresh daily using traditional recipes with a modern twist.
